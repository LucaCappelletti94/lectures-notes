\subsection{Exercise 2 resolution}
We express the problem in terms of $f_1-f_2$:
\begin{align*}
  \max x_1 = f_1 + f_2 \\
  \max x_2 = f_2       \\
  f_1 + 2f_2 & \leq 3  \\
  f_1 + f_2  & \leq 2  \\
  f_1 + f_2  & \geq 0  \\
  f_2        & \geq 0
\end{align*}

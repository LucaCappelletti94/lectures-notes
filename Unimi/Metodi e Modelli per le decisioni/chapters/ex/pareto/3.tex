\subsection{Exercise 3 resolution}
We express the problem in terms of $f_1-f_2$:
\begin{align*}
  x_1  & = \frac{4 f_2  - 2\pm \sqrt{-4 f_2^2 + 24 f_2 + 5 f_1 + 4}}{5}  \\
  x_2  & = \frac{f_2+ 2 -( \pm \sqrt{-4 f_2^2 + 24 f_2 + 5 f_1 + 4})}{5} \\
  \bmx & \geq 0
\end{align*}
